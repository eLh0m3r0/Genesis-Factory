# Genesis Factory — Self-Improvement

## What
The factory itself. Agents, commands, heartbeat, workflows — everything
that makes the autonomous IT team work.

## Why
The factory must continuously improve. Better agents, smarter decisions,
fewer failures, lower costs.

## Direction
- Improve agent reliability (fewer stuck stories)
- Better UAT strategies (stable selectors, data-testid usage)
- Cost optimization (use Sonnet where Opus isn't needed)
- Better morning briefs (trends, comparisons)
- Heartbeat: new monitor types, better error recovery
